Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-01

Summary

TryNameGenerator.com is privacy-first. Name generation runs in your browser; we don’t store your generated names on our servers.

Data We Process

  • No account data: We don’t require sign-up or personal details.
  • Local features: Favorites and settings are saved locally in your browser (LocalStorage) and never sent to us.
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand overall site usage (e.g., pages viewed, approximate location, device type). This helps us improve the site.

Cookies & Analytics

Google Analytics may set cookies to measure anonymous usage. You can block or clear cookies in your browser settings. We don’t sell or share personal data.

Data Retention

We don’t store generated names. Any locally saved favorites remain only in your browser until you clear them.

Your Choices

  • Use your browser’s settings to block analytics cookies.
  • Clear site data (LocalStorage) to remove saved favorites.
  • Use private/incognito mode for additional privacy.
